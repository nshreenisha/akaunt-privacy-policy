Privacy Policy for Akaunt

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Akaunt. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Akaunt may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and business details you provide during registration
  • Business Data: Sales, inventory, customer, and transaction data you enter into the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Camera Access: Used only for scanning barcodes and capturing product images (only when you explicitly use these features)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Akaunt service
  • Process your business transactions and manage inventory
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications about your account
  • Provide customer support

4. Camera Permission

Akaunt requests camera permission for the following purposes:

  • Barcode Scanning: To quickly add products by scanning barcodes
  • Product Images: To capture and attach images to your products

Important: Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to scan a barcode or take a product photo. We do not access your camera in the background or without your permission.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your business data remains private and is only accessible to you and authorized users of your account.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data as long as your account exists or as necessary to provide the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your business data
  • Revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings

9. Children's Privacy

Akaunt is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
